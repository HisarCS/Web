Scratch

After organizing seperate Scratch Day events for 2 years, we decided to join it with other programming languages under Hisar Coding Summit. Scratch, an programming environemnt being developed by MIT, incorporates a "drag and drop" styled coding interface. This interface makes Scratch ideal environment for beginners who would like to start learing more about programming. For our Scratch workshop, we kindly request our guests to bring their own laptops with them.

Processing & Arduino

Processing is a programming language that is very striped out and simplified relative to other languages. Instead of Scratch and App Inventor's "drag and drop" styled programming Processing uses a more tranditional approach by being a text based langauge. It is suggested by many as the ideal language to acquire the basic concepts of programming. Arduino language on the other hand is a modified version of Processing dedicated for electronics. Our guests in this workshop will be able to work on both electronics and coding. We kindly request our guests to bring their own laptops for this workshop.

Discover

App Inventor

App Inventor, an app developed by MIT again, is an enviroment for creating Android apps without any prior knowledge. App Inventor also uses a "drag and drop" based user interface, just like Scratch. Laptops and Android Devices are not mandatory, but suggested for this workshop.
